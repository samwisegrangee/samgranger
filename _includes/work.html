I've always been a jack of trades and master of some

A background in art, photography, and poetry gave me an attentiveness to notice the things around me and the skill needed to translating them into a new medium to share with others. Drawing has been in my life ever since I could hold a pencil. Translating the contours of a plant's leaves or the intricacies of a person's expression into the lines on my paper was a training in attention.
