Madcap Coffee

Madcap Coffee is an award-winning roastery located in Grand Rapids, MI. This site was designed by Conduit Studios. I was brought on board with the CurlyHost development team.
The majority of my work was developing responsive templates and plugins that were intuitive for both visitors to experience and the client to use. Some of my favorite projects were keeping backgrounds lined up with bag labels, custom gradients, generating related products and slideshows on the fly, and responsively dealing with overflow text.
